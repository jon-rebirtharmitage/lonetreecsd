To connect to the public wireless at Lone Tree follow these instructions.

1. You will need to click the following link to download the certiicate.

a. Click on this linke for Apple Products like iPhone and iPads and older Androids

  1. After downloading the cert file should launch a screen that asks you to install a cert. You may have to click yes/install two or three times.
  2. Note that some iOS versions require an extra step to enable the cert. Please go to Settings – General – About and at the bottom is a setting that says Certificate Trust Settings
  3. If you can click that, you should see one called lonetree and just slide it to turn it on.

b. Click on this linke for Newer Android Products Like the Pixel

  1. Open your device's Settings app Settings app.
  2. Tap Security
  3. location and then Encryption
  4. credentials.
  5. Under "Credential storage," tap Install from storage.
  6. Under "Open from," tap where you saved the certificate. Filename will be ng.pem
  7. Tap the file. (If needed, enter the key store password. Tap OK.)
  8. Type a name for the certificate. Lonetree is recommended
  9. Pick VPN and apps or Wi-Fi. and Tap Ok
